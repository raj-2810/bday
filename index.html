STORY of Bhai and Behen



First Meet

To Behen hum mile the 10th June 2019. Bluedoor ko to bhula nahi ja skta Credits: Mekhala Singh. Aur baki ka to aap janti hi ho



Second Meet

To hum mile the 13th Dec 2019.Pata nhi tha 5 months bad milenge aur jyada kuch lagega nahi. Iss bar thoda badh b gya thandi b thi aur kafi jyada mile. behen ne apne dosto se b milaya. Batao log kabir singh chila rhe the kon bhul skta plus behen aur mene sath me panipuri b khayi.



Third Meet

Bahut frustration kisko pata tha ki lockdown aa jayega hum 1 saal tk nahi mil payenge and kiita sara socha tha bich me meil leneg par mil nhin paye. But best part pyar kam nhi hua. and finally 1 saal bad 3rd Jan 2021 ko mile. Itne din bad mile phir b kisi k karan bakwas ho gya aur khush hoke b dard me nikla ye meet.But usme bhi humne khud k moments bana hi liye Aur aunty ne bahut pareshan b kiya tha agrden wali.



Fourth Meet

Pata nhi tha itta jaldi phir milenege though 3 mahine bad hi mile the(15 march 2021).Start me behen gussa ho gyi thi par thik h and also ladki chila k gyi. Aur sath me pizza khaye aur soye bhi. Aur behen ne mere sath Hookah b piya.



Fifth Meet

Phirse kafi tym bad mile almost 4 months bad 17 July 2021 ko. Sath me temple gye the behenn kabhi socha tha lol. Or behen ne Shivnath b dikhaya aur kuch bolna nhi chahega me hihihihi.Also Haveli cafe was good behen. Sath me Cad b me bhukho ki trh pasta b khaye.



Sixth Meet

To abhi tk itna hi mile h aur last hum mile kuch din pehle 10 november 2021 ko. Iss bar bina chuchu k help hum hag diye aur hume kahin ghum hi nhi paye but still not bad hum khate pite susta k maje kiye lol.
